About Us

Atlanta House Buyers buys houses from people in situations just like yours and solve their real estate problems.

We know that trying to sell your own home can be a major hassle! There are Real Estate Contracts that can tie you to commissions, even if you sell the house without the help of an Agent. Listing a house in the newspaper and other publications can be terribly expensive, even if for only a few days. And, after it is all over and the advertisers and Agents take all your money, you may walk away with nothing in your pocket.

I am not a Realtor, just a private individual, like you. If you have never tried to sell a home, you may not understand all that's involved: the commissions, the loan approval time, the waiting for agent to show your house, the ads that never seem to make the phone ring-- the list goes on. Having strangers walking through every room trying to find the smallest thing to bring down your commission inflated asking price.... in fact, it can easily turn into a horrible experience.

I do know what homeowners go through when they try to sell their home. You may still be in the "thinking about it" stage. Maybe you've already tried listing your house with an agent... maybe you haven't decided you're ready to go that route yet.
